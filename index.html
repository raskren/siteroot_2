Since 1993

WIN has worked on hundreds of projects…providing strategy, program design, market development, revenue growth, and information technology consulting services. WIN’s list of clients includes universities, economic development organizations, social service agencies, workforce investment boards, workforce training intermediaries, industry associations, government agencies, and private businesses.

Impact

WIN has helped clients generate over $77m in earned revenue and grants and has directly managed over $63m in economic, workforce, industrial and environmental projects.

Our consulting approach is to match a unique WIN Team to each project and have that team fully engage with a client to analyze and identify strategies, build organizational infrastructure, and implement creative solutions that evolve and improve based on models of performance outcomes and analysis.

Client Engagement

Our client engagement model is built around three guiding principles:

  • Systematically involve people and organizations in the process of identifying and developing common purpose and direction.
  • Build solutions using contemporary tools and innovative methods of continuous performance improvement and customer satisfaction.
  • Networking to increase the magnitude and velocity of change by linking stakeholders to create positive benefit for all.
